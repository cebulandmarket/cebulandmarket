Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CebuLandMarket, we may collect the following information:

  • Sellers: Name, phone number, email address, property details, photos, and documents submitted through our listing form.
  • Buyers: Name, phone number, and message when you submit an inquiry through our inquiry form.
  • All visitors: Basic analytics data such as pages visited, browser type, and general location (city/country level only).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and publish property listings
  • Connect buyers with property sellers
  • Verify property documents for listing approval
  • Send listing-related notifications via email
  • Improve our website and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following cases:

  • Between buyers and sellers: When a buyer submits an inquiry, their contact details are shared with the property seller to facilitate communication.
  • Service providers: We use Formspree to process form submissions. Your form data passes through their servers.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for analytics purposes. These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external services such as Google Maps, Viber, WhatsApp, and Facebook Messenger. These third-party services have their own privacy policies that we do not control.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request removal of your property listing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at cebulandmarket@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

CebuLandMarket is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: cebulandmarket@gmail.com
