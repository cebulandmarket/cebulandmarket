About Tempo

Tempo is a tiny, beautiful calendar that lives on your device.

  • Private by design. Everything stays in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
  • Works offline. Install it as an app from your browser menu.
  • Syncs to any calendar. Export to Apple, Google, Outlook with one tap.
  • Reminders that actually fire. Browser notifications + downloadable .ics with OS-level alarms.

Made with care. Free, forever.
