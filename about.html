About CebuLandMarket

Connecting property owners and buyers across Cebu Province since 2025.

Our Mission

CebuLandMarket was created to make buying and selling land and properties in Cebu easier, more transparent, and more accessible. We believe that every property owner deserves a platform to reach genuine buyers without the high costs of traditional real estate advertising.

We focus on home owners — no agents, no middlemen. Property owners list and sell directly to buyers. You keep full control of your sale, set your own price, and deal with buyers personally. CebuLandMarket simply provides the platform and verifies the documents to keep everything legit.

What We Do

We provide an affordable online listing platform where property owners in Cebu can showcase their lots, land, houses, and commercial properties. For just ₱500 per listing, your property gets exposure to thousands of potential buyers searching online.

Why Choose Us?

Focused on Cebu: We specialize in Cebu Province, covering all municipalities from Cebu City to the southern and northern towns.

Direct Contact: Buyers contact you directly through Messenger, Viber, or WhatsApp. No middleman fees on the sale.

Easy to Use: Simple submission form, quick review process, and your listing goes live within 24 hours.

Pricing

Listing Fee (₱500): One-time fee to get your property listed. Your listing stays active until your property is sold. If your listing does not pass our document verification, your ₱500 is fully refunded.

If a buyer is interested and wants to visit the property, the owner will be the one to show and tour them around. CebuLandMarket connects you with buyers — you handle the viewing personally.

Title & Paperwork Processing

We have a dedicated processor who can handle the title transfer and all necessary paperwork for your property transaction. This includes title transfer, deed of sale, tax declarations, and other documents needed to complete the sale.

The processing fee is ₱50,000 per property, paid by the seller directly to the processor. This is optional — if you already know how to process the paperwork yourself, you're free to do it on your own. But if you want a hassle-free experience, our processor is here to help.

Document Verification (Due Diligence)

We take document verification seriously. Our agents review all property papers before any listing goes live. This protects buyers and keeps our platform trustworthy. Properties with clean, straightforward paperwork get approved within 24-48 hours.

If your documents are incomplete, complicated, or have unresolved issues, we will not approve the listing and your ₱500 listing fee will be fully refunded. No hassle, no risk.

Not sure if your papers are ready? Contact us first — we'll give you a free assessment before you submit.
