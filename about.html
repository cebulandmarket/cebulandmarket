About CebuLandMarket

Connecting property owners and buyers across Cebu Province since 2026.

Our Mission

CebuLandMarket was created to make buying and selling land and properties in Cebu easier, more transparent, and more accessible. We believe that every property owner deserves a platform to reach genuine buyers without the high costs of traditional real estate advertising.

We focus on property owners — no agents, no middlemen. Property owners list and sell on their own terms. You keep full control of your sale and set your own price. Buyer inquiries come through the platform and get forwarded directly to you.

What We Do

We provide a trusted online listing platform where property owners in Cebu can showcase their lots, land, houses, and commercial properties. Your property gets its own dedicated page with exposure to thousands of potential buyers searching online — all with document verification to keep transactions safe and legitimate.

Your Property Gets Its Own Page

Every approved listing on CebuLandMarket gets a property story — a dedicated page just for your property. We don't just post your listing. We share your story. Why you love this place, what you imagined for it, what makes it special.

Because selling a property isn't about giving up on a dream. It's about passing that dream on to someone who'll see what you see and carry it forward. That's what we believe, and that's what we do for every property owner who lists with us.

Why Choose Us?

Sell Smarter, Not Harder: Stop printing tarpaulins and posting on random Facebook groups hoping someone sees it. One listing with us reaches more serious buyers than months of DIY marketing.

Direct from Owners: Every property is listed by the actual owner. No brokers, no agents, no hidden fees.

Selling Land Is Personal: It's not like selling a phone or a car. It's part of your family. It's where you planned to build something someday. We get that. And we make sure the process feels right — not rushed, not shady, not stressful. Just honest.

Safe Transactions: Document verification and due diligence on every listing keeps things legitimate and trustworthy.

More Than Just a Listing

Anybody can upload a photo and slap a price on it. That's not what we do. We dig into the paperwork, walk through the details, and craft a page that shows buyers exactly what they're getting. No surprises, no guesswork. When someone inquires, they already know your property is the real deal.

Manage Your Listing

Already have an active listing? If you need to update your price, photos, description, or any details — just contact us and we'll make the changes for you.
