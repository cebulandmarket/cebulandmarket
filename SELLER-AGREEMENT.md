## 2. Listing Fee

2.1. The Seller pays a **one-time listing fee of ₱100** via GCash to
**0968 751 2330 (account name: CebuLandMarket)** before the Property is
published.

2.2. The listing fee covers intake and document verification (due
diligence). It is **non-refundable once the listing is approved and
published**.

2.3. **Refund guarantee:** If CLM cannot approve the listing after
document verification (e.g., title issues, incomplete papers), the
Seller receives a **full ₱100 refund**.

## 3. Document Verification (Due Diligence)

3.1. The Seller must submit, before listing: a copy of the Transfer/
Original Certificate of Title (or equivalent), the latest Tax
Declaration and Tax Clearance, a valid government-issued ID, and — if
selling on behalf of another — a notarized Special Power of Attorney or
Authority to Sell.

3.2. CLM verifies these documents in good faith. Verification confirms
documents were submitted and reviewed; it is **not a legal guarantee of
title** and does not replace the buyer's own due diligence or a
lawyer's title examination.

## 4. Platform Service Fee (1%)

4.1. A **platform service fee equal to one percent (1%) of the asking
price** applies. This 1% is added to the price displayed to buyers; the
Seller still receives their full base asking price.

4.2. Upon a completed sale of the Property to a buyer introduced through
CLM, the Seller acknowledges that the 1% platform fee belongs to CLM and
authorizes its settlement at closing via GCash, with proof of payment
(GCash screenshot) provided to **info@cebulandmarket.com**.

## 5. Anti-Circumvention

5.1. If a buyer is introduced, referred, or connected to the Seller
through CLM, the **1% platform fee remains due to CLM** even if the
parties attempt to complete the transaction privately, off-platform, or
through a third party, for a period of **twelve (12) months** from the
date of introduction.

5.2. Deliberately bypassing CLM to avoid the platform fee is a material
breach of this Agreement and may result in delisting and legal action
to recover the fee.

## 6. Seller Warranties

The Seller warrants that: (a) they are the lawful registered owner of
the Property or a duly authorized representative; (b) all documents and
information provided are genuine, accurate, and complete; (c) the
Property is free from undisclosed liens, encumbrances, adverse claims,
or pending litigation; and (d) the Property is not simultaneously the
subject of an exclusive sale mandate with another broker that conflicts
with this listing.

## 7. Listing Duration & Showings

7.1. The listing stays active until the Property is sold or the Seller
requests removal in writing (email to info@cebulandmarket.com). There
is **no expiration**.

7.2. CLM (or its owner) personally accompanies interested buyers to view
the Property. The Seller agrees to cooperate reasonably in scheduling
and providing access for viewings.

## 8. Optional Title & Paperwork Processing

CLM offers an **optional** title transfer and paperwork processing
service for a separate fee of **₱50,000**, paid by the Seller directly,
independent of the listing fee and platform fee. This service is
elected at the Seller's discretion and is not required to list.

## 9. No Guarantee of Sale

CLM is a listing and marketing platform and facilitator. CLM does not
buy the Property and does not guarantee that the Property will sell
within any particular timeframe or at any particular price.

## 10. Misrepresentation

Submission of falsified documents or materially false information
results in **immediate delisting, forfeiture of the listing fee, and
possible civil or criminal action**. The Seller indemnifies CLM against
any claim arising from the Seller's misrepresentation.

## 11. Liability

The sale contract is solely between the Seller and the buyer. CLM is
not a party to the deed of sale and is not liable for the parties'
performance, payment, or post-sale disputes beyond its document-
verification and introduction role.

## 12. Data Privacy

The Seller consents to CLM collecting, storing, and processing the
Seller's information, and to publicly displaying the Property details,
in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173). Personal
contact details are recorded in CLM's internal seller records and are
not published publicly without consent.

## 13. Non-Exclusive

This listing is **non-exclusive**. The Seller may market the Property
elsewhere but must notify CLM in writing within 24 hours once the
Property is sold or withdrawn so the listing can be updated.

## 14. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice. The
Seller may request listing removal at any time. Fees already earned
under Sections 4 and 5 survive termination.

## 15. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of the
Philippines. Any dispute shall be brought before the proper courts of
**Cebu**.
