Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CebuLandMarket

For Buyers

Is CebuLandMarket legit?

Yes. CebuLandMarket is a property listing platform based in Cebu, Philippines. We verify all property documents before any listing goes live. We only publish listings with clean and complete paperwork to protect both buyers and sellers.

Do I need to pay anything as a buyer?

No. Browsing listings and contacting sellers is completely free. The only costs are the standard transaction costs (taxes, notarial fees, transfer fees) which are part of any normal property purchase.

Are the listings verified?

Yes. Our team reviews the property documents (title, tax declaration, etc.) before publishing any listing. Properties with incomplete or problematic documents are not approved. However, we always recommend that buyers do their own due diligence before purchasing.

How do I view a property?

Send us a message through the contact buttons on the listing page and we'll coordinate a viewing for you. The property owner will personally show you the property and walk you through the land.

Can I negotiate the price?

Yes. The listed price is the seller's asking price. If you're interested in negotiating, let us know through the contact buttons and we'll coordinate with the property owner on your behalf.

For Sellers

How much does it cost to list my property?

There is a one-time listing fee of ₱500 via GCash. Your listing stays active until your property is sold — no monthly fees or renewals. If your listing is not approved after document review, the ₱500 is fully refunded.

What documents do I need?

At minimum, you need a Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) or Tax Declaration, plus a valid government ID. Having complete and clean documents speeds up the approval process. Properties with ongoing legal issues, missing documents, or complicated ownership will not be approved.

How long before my listing goes live?

After submitting your listing and paying the ₱500 fee, our team reviews your documents within 24-48 hours. If everything is in order, your listing goes live immediately after approval.

What if my listing is not approved?

If your property does not pass our document verification, your ₱500 listing fee is fully refunded via GCash. We will inform you of the reason for non-approval so you can fix the issue and resubmit if you wish.

Do I need to show the property to buyers myself?

Yes. When a buyer is interested, they contact CebuLandMarket first — not you directly. We handle the inquiry, coordinate the schedule, and then let you know when to show the property. All you need to do is be available and show the buyer around your land.

What does the ₱50,000 processor fee cover?

The ₱50,000 covers our processor's service fee for handling all the paperwork — from deed of sale up to title transfer. Government fees, taxes, notarial fees, and other charges required during the process are separate and are the obligation of the seller and buyer as agreed upon. This service is optional.

General

How do I contact CebuLandMarket?

You can reach us via email at cebulandmarket@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Is CebuLandMarket a real estate broker?

No. CebuLandMarket is an online listing platform that connects property sellers with potential buyers. We are not a real estate broker or agent. All transactions are between the buyer and seller directly. We provide document verification to ensure listing quality.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all municipalities and cities in Cebu Province, including Cebu City, Mandaue, Lapu-Lapu, Talisay, Minglanilla, Naga, Carcar, Argao, Dalaguete, Moalboal, Barili, Ronda, Oslob, Santander, and many more.
