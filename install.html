Install CebuLandMarket

Browse property listings offline — no WiFi needed

If the button doesn't work, follow the steps below for your device.

Why install?

  • Works offline — browse listings even without internet
  • Faster — opens instantly like a real app
  • Home screen icon — one tap to open, no browser needed
  • No app store needed — install directly from the website
  • Free — no charge, no storage wasted

📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

🍎

How to install on iPhone or iPad

  1. Open cebulandmarket.com in Safari (not Chrome or Facebook browser)
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  5. Done! CebuLandMarket is now on your home screen

🤖 Android (Chrome)

🤖

How to install on Android

  1. Open cebulandmarket.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top-right corner
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Install" to confirm
  5. Done! CebuLandMarket is now on your home screen

💻 Desktop / Laptop (Chrome or Edge)

💻

How to install on Computer

  1. Open cebulandmarket.com in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar (right side)
  3. Click "Install"
  4. Done! CebuLandMarket opens as a standalone app

💬 Using Facebook Browser?

If you opened this from Facebook or Messenger

  1. Tap the three dots menu (⋯) or "Open in browser"
  2. This will open the page in your phone's real browser (Safari or Chrome)
  3. Then follow the iPhone or Android steps above

Note: You cannot install from the Facebook in-app browser. You must open it in Safari or Chrome first.
