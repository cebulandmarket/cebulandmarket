100% Refund Guarantee

If your listing does not pass our document verification, your ₱500 will be fully refunded via GCash. No risk to you — we only keep the fee if your property is approved and published.

1% Platform Fee

We add 1% from your asking price in the listing. There's nothing deducted from your asking price — you still receive the full amount you set.

If a buyer is interested and wants to visit the property, you (the owner) will be the one to show and tour them around. We connect you with buyers — you handle the viewing personally.

Taxes, notarial, and other transfer costs are handled between the buyer and seller — same as any normal property transaction.

Title & Paperwork Processing

We have a dedicated processor who can handle the title transfer and all necessary paperwork for your transaction.

Processing fee: ₱50,000 per property, paid by the seller directly to the processor.

This is optional — if you can process the paperwork yourself, you're free to do it on your own. But if you want a hassle-free experience, our processor is here to help.

Document Verification (Due Diligence)

Our agents review all property documents before any listing goes live. This protects both sellers and buyers on our platform.

We approve listings with:

  • Clean title (TCT/OCT) under seller's name
  • Updated tax declarations and receipts
  • No legal disputes or encumbrances
  • Clear ownership, no complicated transfers

We will NOT approve:

  • Properties with ongoing legal cases
  • Complicated ownership (multiple heirs, no settlement)
  • Fake or incomplete documents
  • Properties with unpaid taxes (3+ years)
  • Mortgaged properties without bank clearance

If your listing is not approved, your ₱500 is refunded in full.
