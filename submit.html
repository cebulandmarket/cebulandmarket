Before You Start

Please read and agree to the following terms before submitting your property listing.

We'll Create a Personal Page for Your Property

Every approved listing gets its own dedicated page — a personal website that tells your property's story. Your vision, your memories, what makes the land special. We share it so the right buyer can see what you see.

Selling isn't giving up on a dream. It's sharing it with someone who'll carry it forward.

Listing Fee: ₱500

A one-time listing fee of ₱500 is required to publish your property on CebuLandMarket. Your listing stays active until your property is sold.

If your listing does not pass our document verification, your ₱500 will be fully refunded.

1% Platform Fee — You Choose How

CebuLandMarket charges a 1% platform fee on every successful sale. You get to choose how it's handled:

  • Option A: We add 1% on top of your asking price — buyers see a slightly higher listing price, but you receive your full asking price.
  • Option B: The 1% is already included in the price you give — the listing shows your exact price, and you remit 1% to us upon sale.

Selling Per Cut? If you have a large lot, you can choose to sell portions (per sqm). The 1% fee applies to each cut/transaction.

Upon successful sale, the 1% platform fee must be remitted to CebuLandMarket via GCash within 7 days of closing the transaction.

Title & Paperwork Processing (Optional)

We have a dedicated processor who can handle the title transfer and all necessary paperwork for your property transaction. This includes title transfer, deed of sale, tax declarations, and other documents needed to complete the sale.

The processing fee is ₱50,000 per property, paid by the seller directly to the processor. This covers the processor's service only — running all the paperwork from deed of sale up to title transfer. Government fees, taxes, notarial fees, and other charges required during the process are separate and are the obligation of the seller and buyer as agreed upon.

Need it faster? We also offer expedited processing if you're in a rush. An additional fee will apply depending on the timeline needed.

This service is optional — if you already know how to process the paperwork yourself, you're free to do it on your own. But if you want a hassle-free experience, our processor is here to help.

Document Verification (Due Diligence)

We take document verification seriously. Our team reviews all property papers before any listing goes live. This protects buyers and keeps our platform trustworthy. Properties with clean, straightforward paperwork get approved within 24-48 hours.

If your documents are incomplete, complicated, or have unresolved issues, we will not approve the listing and your ₱500 listing fee will be fully refunded. No hassle, no risk.

Not sure if your papers are ready? Contact us first — we'll give you a free assessment before you submit.

Transaction Costs

All other costs (taxes, notarial fees, transfer fees, etc.) are handled between the buyer and seller — same as any normal property transaction.
