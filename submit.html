Before You Start

Please read and agree to the following terms before submitting your property listing.

Listing Fee: ₱500

A one-time listing fee of ₱500 is required to publish your property on CebuLandMarket. Your listing stays active until your property is sold.

If your listing does not pass our document verification, your ₱500 will be fully refunded.

1% Platform Fee

We add 1% from your asking price in the listing. There's nothing deducted from your asking price — you still receive the full amount you set.

Property Viewing

If a buyer is interested and wants to visit the property, you (the owner) will be the one to show and tour them around.

Title & Paperwork Processing (Optional)

We have a dedicated processor who can handle title transfer and paperwork for ₱50,000 per property, paid directly to the processor. If you can process the paperwork yourself, you're free to do it on your own.

Document Verification

Our team will review your property documents before your listing goes live. Properties with clean, complete paperwork get approved within 24-48 hours. Incomplete or complicated documents will not be approved, and your ₱500 will be refunded.

Transaction Costs

All other costs (taxes, notarial fees, transfer fees, etc.) are handled between the buyer and seller — same as any normal property transaction.
