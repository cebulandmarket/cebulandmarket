Title & Paperwork Processing (Optional)

We have a dedicated processor who can handle the title transfer and all necessary paperwork for your property transaction. This includes title transfer, deed of sale, tax declarations, and other documents needed to complete the sale.

The processing fee is ₱50,000 per property, paid by the seller directly to the processor. This covers the processor's service only — running all the paperwork from deed of sale up to title transfer. Government fees, taxes, notarial fees, and other charges required during the process are separate and are the obligation of the seller and buyer as agreed upon.

This service is optional — if you already know how to process the paperwork yourself, you're free to do it on your own. But if you want a hassle-free experience, our processor is here to help.

Document Verification (Due Diligence)

We take document verification seriously. Our team reviews all property papers before any listing goes live. This protects buyers and keeps our platform trustworthy. Properties with clean, straightforward paperwork get approved within 24-48 hours.

If your documents are incomplete, complicated, or have unresolved issues, we will not approve the listing and your ₱100 listing fee will be fully refunded. No hassle, no risk.

Not sure if your papers are ready? Contact us first — we'll give you a free assessment before you submit.

Transaction Costs

All other costs (taxes, notarial fees, transfer fees, etc.) are handled between the buyer and seller — same as any normal property transaction.
