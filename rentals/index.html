CebuRentMarket is just a platform — a website where landlords or property owners can post their house, room, condo, or apartment for rent. We are not real estate agents, not brokers, and we don't charge any hidden fees.

If you're looking for a rental (Tenant):

  • Browsing listings on the website is completely free
  • If you find a property you like, submit an inquiry through our platform
  • We'll connect you with the property owner after your inquiry
  • You can visit the property in person before renting — but we are not agents, we won't accompany you on viewings
  • You and the landlord arrange everything directly — we don't get involved
  • Once you finalize your rental, a signed rental agreement will be provided by CebuRentMarket — this is your legal protection as a tenant.
  • Please note: If you proceed with renting without going through CebuRentMarket's process, we cannot guarantee your protection or provide the rental agreement.

If you're renting out a property (Landlord):

  • Just submit your property details on our website
  • Once approved, your property will be seen by thousands of tenants online
  • Worldwide reach! OFWs, business people outside the Philippines, and anyone looking for rentals in Cebu can view and install our platform from anywhere in the world
  • You set your own rental price
  • Check the agreement before listing your property
  • A rental agreement will be provided — protecting you legally from day one. Once a tenant is found, notify CebuRentMarket so we can finalize the agreement.
  • Our platform fee is only collected after a successful rental — you pay nothing upfront.

What we don't do:

  • We don't clean the property or unit
  • We are not caretakers or property managers
  • We don't collect monthly rent — you arrange that yourselves
  • We don't guarantee tenant behavior after move-in
  • We don't resolve disputes between landlord and tenant
  • We don't conduct property inspections or ocular visits
  • We don't verify the physical condition of the property
  • We don't provide legal advice to either party
  • We don't act as guarantor for unpaid rent
  • We don't handle utility transfers or setup (water, electricity, internet)
  • We don't renew contracts automatically — renewal is between landlord and tenant directly

Tips to stay safe:

  • Always visit the property in person before renting — so you can see it for yourself
  • When meeting the landlord, bring someone with you
  • Never pay if you haven't visited the unit and aren't sure yet
  • Read the contract or lease agreement before signing
  • Clarify how much the deposit, advance, and monthly rent are
  • Ask if any utilities are included (water, electricity, wifi)
  • Take photos of the unit before moving in — so you have proof of its condition
  • If you have any doubts or questions, just message us — it's free, we'll help you
