3. How the Platform Works

For Landlords:

  • Submit a rental listing through our website with complete details and photos.
  • Listing your rental property is FREE — no listing fee required.
  • CebuRentMarket verifies the property (ownership, existence, photos, lease terms).
  • If approved, the listing is published on cebulandmarket.com/rentals/ and remains active until rented or removed by the landlord.

  • Upon a successful rental deal facilitated through our platform, the Landlord pays a platform fee.

  • All rental agreements are between landlord and tenant directly. CebuRentMarket is not a party to the lease.

5. Rental Listing Guidelines

When submitting a rental listing, Landlords must:

  • Provide accurate, truthful, and complete information about the rental property.
  • Submit authentic photos that accurately represent the current condition of the property.
  • Be the lawful owner or authorized representative of the property.
  • Disclose the monthly rent, required deposits, and any additional fees (utilities, association dues, etc.).
  • Not list properties involved in legal disputes (unless disclosed).
  • Not misrepresent the condition, amenities, or features of the property.

CebuRentMarket reserves the right to reject or remove any listing that contains false information, violates these terms, or fails our verification process.
