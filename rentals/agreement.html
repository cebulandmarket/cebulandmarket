  19. Pre-Termination

    By the LESSEE: 30 days written notice required. The security deposit is automatically forfeited — no refund. The LESSEE is still liable for unpaid rent, utilities, and damages.

    By the LESSOR: The LESSOR may terminate this lease if the LESSEE:

    1. Fails to pay rent on time
    2. Sublets or has unauthorized occupants
    3. Uses the property for illegal or immoral purposes
    4. Causes significant damage through negligence
    5. Violates any other term of this contract

    The LESSOR gives 15 days to fix the violation. If not fixed, the lease is terminated and deposit is forfeited.

  20. Abandonment

    If the LESSEE abandons the property before the lease ends, the deposit is forfeited and the LESSEE must pay all remaining rent and damages. The LESSOR may enter the property and lease it to someone else.

  21. Surrender of Property

    When the lease ends, the LESSEE must vacate immediately and return the property in the same condition (normal wear and tear accepted). Return all keys, access cards, and remotes. Items left after   days may be disposed of.

  22. Lessor's Non-Liability

    The LESSEE shall hold the LESSOR free and harmless from any loss, damage, or injury suffered by the LESSEE, members of his household, guests, or other third persons arising from the use of the Leased Premises. The LESSOR shall not be liable or responsible for:

    1. The presence of bugs, vermin, rats, insects, or other similar creatures in the Leased Premises
    2. Failure of electrical and/or water supply
    3. Destruction or damage to the plumbing system, water closet, or water pipes in, above, or about the Leased Premises
    4. Any accident, injury, death, loss, or damage which the LESSEE, members of his household, employees, or guests might sustain while in the Leased Premises
    5. Any damage arising from the plumbing, gas, water, or air-conditioning system or from the bursting, leaking, or destruction of any tank, cistern, or pipes
    6. Losses or damages occasioned by events beyond control such as fire, earthquake, typhoon, flood, volcanic eruption, robbery, theft, or other crimes

    No compensation, claim, extension of term, or suspension of rental shall be allowed against the LESSOR by reason of the LESSEE's claim of inconvenience, annoyance, or injury for any reason.

  23. Force Majeure

    If the property is destroyed by fire, earthquake, typhoon, flood, or other uncontrollable events, this lease automatically ends. Neither party is liable.

  24. Liability for Suits

    The LESSEE holds the LESSOR harmless from any legal actions, claims, or demands arising from the LESSEE's use of the property or violation of any laws or terms of this contract.

  25. Breach of Contract, Lessor's Lien and Power of Attorney

    All covenants and agreements in this contract shall be deemed essential conditions. Any default or breach of any term or condition shall be sufficient ground for termination of this lease and shall give the LESSOR the right to:

    1. Terminate this lease and enter upon the premises
    2. Without need of demand or resort to judicial proceedings, expel the LESSEE or padlock the premises including the LESSEE's effects found inside the Leased Premises
    3. Forfeit the security deposit and advance rental in favor of the LESSOR by way of liquidated damages
    4. Pursue claims for any unpaid rent, utilities, penalties, and damages

    The LESSEE hereby gives the LESSOR full prior consent and authority to use all necessary and reasonable force to disconnect facilities and padlock the premises. Any enforcement of this provision by the LESSOR shall not be regarded as trespass or considered unlawful.

    The LESSOR shall have the right to transfer the LESSEE's properties to any available space under the LESSOR's control, with the right to charge the LESSEE reasonable rental for the use of such space.

    Should the LESSEE fail to pay all outstanding obligations within fifteen (15) days from expiration, termination, or eviction, the LESSOR is hereby constituted as the LESSEE's SPECIAL ATTORNEY-IN-FACT, with authority and power to sell, dispose, or retain any or all of the LESSEE's goods, furniture, equipment, and other property found in the premises, or such portion as would be sufficient to secure funds to satisfy arrearages, unpaid obligations, costs of removal, handling, and other charges. All such costs shall be for the account of the LESSEE.

    This Special Power of Attorney shall continue to subsist for as long as the LESSEE has unpaid obligations arising from this contract, it being an integral part hereof. The LESSEE hereby expressly gives consent to and ratifies any and all acts performed by the LESSOR in the exercise of this power.

    The LESSEE agrees that the remedies herein provided are cumulative and not exclusive of any remedies provided by law.

  27. Cumulative Remedies

    The LESSOR's delay in enforcing any right does not waive it. All rights under this contract and under the law remain available.
