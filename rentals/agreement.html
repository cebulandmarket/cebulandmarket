  9. NO SUBLEASING

    The LESSEE is strictly prohibited from subleasing, assigning, or transferring occupancy of any portion of the Leased Premises to any third party, whether through formal agreement or informal arrangement, without the LESSOR's prior written approval. The LESSEE is likewise prohibited from listing or advertising the Leased Premises on Airbnb or any similar short-term rental platform. Any breach of this provision shall constitute a material violation of this Contract, entitling the LESSOR to immediately terminate the lease and forfeit the advance rent and security deposit as liquidated damages.

  10. ALTERATIONS AND IMPROVEMENTS

    No structural modifications, alterations, or improvements shall be made to the Leased Premises without the LESSOR's prior written approval. Any permanent improvements or alterations that cannot be removed without causing damage or defacement to the property shall automatically become the LESSOR's property upon termination of this Contract, with no obligation to reimburse the LESSEE.

    Painting, drilling holes, and any modifications to the electrical or plumbing systems are prohibited without the LESSOR's prior written approval.

  11. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

    The LESSEE shall maintain the Leased Premises in a clean, sanitary, and good condition throughout the lease term. Upon termination or expiration, the LESSEE shall return the property in the same condition as when received, with allowance for normal wear and tear.

    Regular upkeep of appliances and electronics, including quarterly servicing of air-conditioning units by a qualified technician, shall be at the LESSEE's expense.

    Repairs costing   (P  ) or below shall be classified as minor repairs and shall be borne by the LESSEE.

    Repairs exceeding the above threshold shall be classified as major repairs and shall be the LESSOR's responsibility, provided the damage was not caused by the LESSEE's fault, negligence, or that of his household, guests, or employees.

    For major repairs, the LESSEE shall promptly inform the LESSOR of the nature of the damage, and the LESSOR shall undertake the necessary repairs within a reasonable timeframe.

  12. RULES AND REGULATIONS

    The LESSEE shall observe and comply with all applicable rules and regulations of the building management and the homeowner's association, which the LESSEE acknowledges having been informed of prior to entering into this lease.

    Smoking inside the Leased Premises is strictly prohibited. No furniture, appliances, or equipment belonging to the LESSOR shall be removed from the property without the LESSOR's written permission.

  13. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

    The LESSEE shall not store or keep any flammable, combustible, or explosive substances within the Leased Premises, as such storage would be in violation of applicable fire insurance policies. Should this provision be violated, the LESSOR may immediately terminate this Contract, and the LESSEE shall be held fully liable for all resulting damages, without prejudice to any other legal remedies available to the LESSOR.

  14. LEGAL COMPLIANCE

    The LESSEE shall observe and comply with all applicable laws, ordinances, rules, regulations, and licensing or permit requirements pertaining to the use, occupancy, and sanitation of the Leased Premises. Any failure to comply shall be entirely at the LESSEE's risk, cost, and liability, and the LESSOR shall be held harmless from any consequences thereof. The LESSEE shall indemnify the LESSOR against any fines, claims, demands, or legal actions of any nature brought by any authority, entity, or person resulting from the LESSEE's non-compliance.

  15. LIABILITY FOR PROPERTY DAMAGE

    The LESSEE shall bear full responsibility for repairing any damage to the Leased Premises, including all furniture, fixtures, and appliances, that results from or is attributable to the willful or negligent acts of the LESSEE, members of his household, guests, or employees.

    Should any furniture or fixture listed in "Annex A" be lost or damaged, the LESSEE shall compensate the LESSOR based on the item's current replacement value.

  16. RIGHT OF INSPECTION

    The LESSEE shall keep the Leased Premises in habitable condition. The LESSOR reserves the right, upon at least seven (7) days' prior notice, to enter and inspect the Leased Premises at reasonable hours.

    The LESSOR or any authorized representative may, with prior notice, access the Leased Premises at reasonable times to inspect its condition, observe its use, or perform necessary maintenance work.

    During the final month of the lease term, the LESSEE shall permit the LESSOR or authorized representatives to access the Leased Premises for the purpose of showing it to prospective tenants.

  17. LIMITATION OF LESSOR'S LIABILITY

    The LESSEE shall hold the LESSOR free and harmless from any loss, damage, or injury suffered by the LESSEE, members of his household, guests, or any third parties arising from the LESSEE's use of the Leased Premises, including but not limited to claims for property damage, personal injury, or death, as well as losses resulting from unforeseeable events or force majeure including fire, earthquake, lightning, typhoon, flood, volcanic eruption, robbery, theft, or other criminal acts. Furthermore, the LESSOR shall bear no liability for:

    1. The presence of pests, vermin, rodents, insects, or other similar creatures within the Leased Premises
    2. Interruptions or failures in electrical power or water supply
    3. Damage to or malfunction of plumbing systems, cistern tanks, washbasins, toilets, or water pipes within or around the Leased Premises
    4. Any accident, injury, death, loss, or damage sustained by the LESSEE, household members, employees, or guests while on the Leased Premises
    5. Any damage caused by or arising from plumbing, gas, water, pipes, or air-conditioning systems, including the bursting, leaking, or failure of any tank, cistern, valve, toilet, or drainage pipeline within or around the Leased Premises
    6. Losses or damages caused by fire, earthquake, typhoon, flood, volcanic eruption, robbery, theft, or other criminal acts

    The LESSEE shall not be entitled to any compensation, claim for extension of the lease term, or suspension of rental payments on account of any alleged inconvenience, annoyance, or injury for any reason whatsoever.

  18. DESTRUCTION OF PREMISES

    If the Leased Premises or any portion thereof is destroyed by fire, natural disaster, or any other cause beyond the LESSOR's control and without fault on the LESSOR's part, this Contract shall be deemed automatically terminated. The LESSEE shall hold the LESSOR free from any claim for damages or liability in connection with such destruction.

  19. INDEMNIFICATION AGAINST LEGAL CLAIMS

    The LESSEE shall defend and hold the LESSOR harmless from any lawsuit, claim, demand, or proceeding brought by any party arising from the LESSEE's failure to observe or perform any applicable law, regulation, ordinance, or any provision of this Contract, without prejudice to the LESSOR's right to terminate this lease.

  20. ABANDONMENT

    If the LESSEE vacates or abandons the Leased Premises before the lease term expires, the LESSEE shall compensate the LESSOR for all unpaid rent and damages resulting from such abandonment, and the security deposit shall be forfeited in favor of the LESSOR. In such event, the LESSOR shall be entitled to: (a) terminate this Contract; (b) enter and take possession of the Leased Premises, by force if necessary, without incurring any liability; and (c) re-lease the property to a new tenant and collect rent therefrom, without waiving any other right of action for damages available under law or under this Contract.

  21. DEFAULT, LESSOR'S LIEN, AND POWER OF ATTORNEY

    All covenants and agreements contained herein are deemed essential conditions of this lease. Any default or violation shall entitle the LESSOR to terminate this Contract, re-enter the Leased Premises, and without need of demand or judicial action, expel the LESSEE or secure the premises, including any of the LESSEE's belongings found therein. The LESSEE hereby expressly consents to and authorizes the LESSOR to take all reasonable measures necessary to disconnect utilities and secure the premises. No such action by the LESSOR or its representatives shall be deemed trespass or give rise to any legal claim by the LESSEE.

    Upon exercising the right to retake possession, the LESSOR may transfer any of the LESSEE's belongings found in the Leased Premises to any available storage space under the LESSOR's control, and may charge the LESSEE reasonable storage fees not exceeding the monthly rent for the Leased Premises.

    If eviction becomes necessary, or if the LESSEE fails to settle all outstanding obligations within fifteen (15) days from the date of expiration, termination, or eviction, the LESSOR is hereby irrevocably appointed as the LESSEE's SPECIAL ATTORNEY-IN-FACT, with full authority to sell, dispose of, or retain any or all of the LESSEE's goods, furniture, equipment, supplies, and other property found in the premises, in such amounts as may be sufficient to satisfy all arrears, unpaid obligations, removal costs, transportation, handling, and other related expenses, all of which shall be charged to the LESSEE.

    This Special Power of Attorney shall remain in effect for as long as the LESSEE has any unpaid obligation under this Contract. It is an integral part of this agreement, and the LESSEE hereby ratifies and confirms all actions taken by the LESSOR in the exercise of this authority.

  22. SURRENDER OF PREMISES

    Upon expiration or termination of this Contract for any reason, the LESSEE shall immediately vacate and surrender the Leased Premises to the LESSOR in the same condition as when first received, with allowance for normal wear and tear.

  23. JURISDICTION

    All disputes arising from the interpretation or enforcement of this Contract shall be submitted exclusively to the appropriate courts of Cebu City.

  24. WAIVER

    The failure of either party to enforce any provision of this Contract on a particular occasion shall not constitute a waiver of that provision or any other provision in future instances.

  25. CUMULATIVE REMEDIES

    No delay or failure by the LESSOR in exercising any right or remedy under this Contract shall operate as a waiver thereof. No partial exercise of any right shall preclude the further exercise of that or any other right. All remedies available to the LESSOR under this Contract are cumulative and are in addition to any remedies available under applicable law.

  26. SEVERABILITY

    Should any provision of this Contract be declared invalid or unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect. The parties' rights and obligations shall be interpreted as though the invalid provision were not included in this Contract.
