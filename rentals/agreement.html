    The LESSEE shall not directly or indirectly, sublease or assign any part of the leased premises or enter into any contract, agreement or arrangement where another person will be allowed to occupy any area of the leased premises, without the prior written consent of the LESSOR. Consequently, the LESSEE is absolutely prohibited from advertising or offering the leased properties in AIR B & B or any similar platform. Any violation of this provision would be deemed as a gross violation of the contract for which the contract may be immediately terminated at the option of the LESSOR, without prejudice to the forfeiture of the advanced rental and security deposit by way of liquidated damages.

  10. IMPROVEMENTS

    The LESSEE shall not make any structural changes, alterations or improvements in the Leased Premises without the written consent of the LESSOR; however, any major alterations or useful improvements made or introduced by the LESSEE, which cannot be removed without causing any damage or defacement to the Leased Premises shall, upon the termination of this Contract, become property of the LESSOR without need of reimbursing the cost to the LESSEE.

    The LESSEE shall not paint, bore holes on the wall, and make alterations or changes in the electrical, plumbing installations within the leased premises, without the prior written consent and approval of the LESSOR.

  11. SANITATION AND REPAIRS

    The LESSEE shall keep and maintain the Leased Properties in good, clean and sanitary condition during the term of the Contract; and shall surrender the leased properties upon the expiration or termination of the lease, in the same condition when it was received, reasonable wear and tear accepted.

    Routine maintenance of any electronics/appliances such as but not limited to the routine maintenance of the air-conditioning units by a competent technician on a quarterly basis shall also be for the account of the LESSEE.

    It is understood that repairs costing   (P  ) or less, shall be considered minor and shall be for the account of the LESSEE.

    Repairs in excess of the above amount shall be treated as major repairs and shall be for the account of the LESSOR; provided however that the damage is not caused because of the fault or negligence of the LESSEE, his household, guests or employees.

    In case of major repairs, the LESSEE shall immediately notify the LESSOR of the nature of the repairs required and the LESSOR undertakes to repair the same within reasonable time.

  12. COMPLIANCE WITH RULES AND REGULATIONS

    The LESSEE shall comply with any and all rules and regulations of the building and of the homeowner's association; which LESSEE hereby acknowledges have been made known to him, prior to the lease.

    The LESSEE further agrees that smoking inside the leased premises is absolutely prohibited and that no appliances, furniture and equipment owned by LESSOR may be brought out of the property without the prior written consent of the LESSOR.

  13. PROHIBITION ON FIRE HAZARDS

    The LESSEE shall not deposit or store within the leased premises any inflammable or explosive materials or substances, the same being in violation of the fire insurance policy covering said premises. In the event of violation thereof, the LESSOR shall have the right to immediately cancel and pre-terminate the instant contract; provided further that the LESSEE shall be responsible and liable for any and all damage caused to the LESSOR; without prejudice to the other rights of the LESSOR under the law.

  14. UNDERTAKING TO ABIDE BY THE LAW, ORDINANCES, ETC.

    The LESSEE hereby undertakes to abide by all laws, ordinances, rules and regulations, issuances and licensing and permit requirements relative to the use, occupation and sanitation of the leased premises. Non-compliance shall be at the exclusive risk, expense and liability of the LESSEE who holds the LESSOR harmless of such non-compliance. LESSEE further undertakes to indemnify the LESSOR for any and all fines, claims, demands and whatever actions of whatever kind or nature by any entity, authority or person for or by reason of any failure to comply with this obligation on the part of the LESSEE.

  15. RESPONSIBILITY FOR REPAIRS FOR DAMAGES CAUSED BY WILLFUL OR NEGLIGENT ACTS

    The LESSEE shall be responsible for and shall undertake the repair of any and all damages to the leased properties including the furniture, fixtures and appliances, caused by or arising from or attributable to the willful or negligent acts of the LESSEE, his household, guests and employees.

    In the event of loss or damage to the furniture and fixtures listed in "Annex A", the LESSEE agrees to indemnify the LESSOR for the damage or loss based on current price.

  16. LESSOR'S RIGHT OF INSPECTION

    The LESSEE shall maintain the Leased Premises in good and tenantable condition and for such purpose the LESSOR reserves the right at reasonable times, 7 days prior notice, to enter and inspect the Leased Premises.

    The LESSEE agrees that the LESSOR or its authorized representative may at any reasonable times, with prior notice, enter into and upon the LEASED PREMISES to inspect or observe the manner of its use, make necessary repairs for the proper operation or maintenance of the LEASED PREMISES.

    Further, during the last One (1) month of the term of this Contract, LESSEE agrees to allow LESSOR or its authorized representative access to the LEASED PREMISES for purposes of showing it to prospective tenants.

  17. LESSOR'S NON-LIABILITY

    The LESSEE shall hold LESSOR free and harmless from any loss, damage, injury suffered by the LESSEE, the members of his household and his guests or other third persons arising out of the use of the Leased Properties by LESSEE, such as but not limited to, claims for property damage, personal injury or wrongful death, or losses or damages occasioned by reason of any event or cause which could not be foreseen, or which, though foreseen, were inevitable such as but not limited to fire, earthquake, lightning, typhoons, flood, volcanic eruption, robbery, theft or other crimes. In addition, the LESSOR shall not be liable nor responsible:

    1. For the presence of bugs, vermin, rats, insects, or other similar creatures, if any, in the Leased Properties
    2. For the failure of electrical and/or water supply
    3. Destruction or damage to the plumbing system and/or cistern tank, washstands, water closet or water pipe in, above or about the leased premises
    4. For any accident, injury, death, loss or damage which the LESSEE, the members of his household, his employees or guests might sustain while in the Leased Properties
    5. For any damage done or occasioned by, or arising from the plumbing, gas, water, and/or other pipes or air-conditioning system or for the bursting, leaking or destruction of any tank, cistern, washers, and water closets or waste pipelines in, above, upon, or about said Leased Properties
    6. Losses or damages occasioned by fire, earthquake, typhoon, flood, volcanic eruption, robbery, theft or other crimes

    No compensation or claim or extension of term or suspension of rental shall be allowed against the LESSOR by reason of LESSEE's claim of inconvenience, annoyance or injury for any reason.

  18. DESTRUCTION

    Should the entire leased premises or any part thereof be destroyed through fire or fortuitous events or by any cause, independent of the will and without fault of the LESSOR, during the effectivity of this contract, the instant Contract will be deemed automatically terminated. In such case, the LESSEE holds the LESSOR free from any damages or liability with respect to the same.

  19. LIABILITY FOR SUITS

    The LESSEE shall absolve the said LESSOR against all action, suit, damages and claims by whomsoever they may be brought or made by reason of the non-observance or non-performance of any rules, regulations, ordinances or laws or of any of the covenants of this contract, without prejudice to the right of the LESSOR to cancel this Lease Contract.

  20. ABANDONMENT

    Should LESSEE abandon the premises leased before the termination of the period lease, LESSEE shall indemnify the LESSOR for all rentals or damages caused by LESSEE'S abandonment and his deposit is hereby forfeited in favor of the LESSOR. The LESSOR shall have the right to (a) terminate this Contract; (b) enter the LEASED PREMISES either by force or otherwise without being liable therefore; and (c) lease the LEASED PREMISES to another party and to receive the rent therefore, without prejudice to any other right of action for damages in accordance with law or as supplied by this Contract.

  21. BREACH, LESSOR'S LIEN AND POWER OF ATTORNEY

    The parties agree that all the covenants and agreements herein contained shall be deemed to be essential conditions as well as covenants. Any default or breach thereof shall be sufficient ground for the termination of this Contract which will give the LESSOR the right to terminate this lease, enter upon the premises and without need of demand or resort to judicial proceedings, expel the LESSEE or padlock the premises including the LESSEE effects found inside the leased premises. All these acts that LESSOR may do without need of any court order, are hereby agreed upon and consented by the LESSEE, as LESSEE hereby explicitly gives the LESSOR full prior consent and authority to use all necessary and reasonable force to disconnect facilities and padlock the premises. Any enforcement of this provision by the LESSOR or any of its representatives are therefore not to be regarded as trespass or to be sued as such or in any way be considered as unlawful.

    In connection with the right of the LESSOR to take physical possession of the leased premises and the LESSEE'S properties found therein, the LESSOR shall have the right to transfer the LESSEE'S properties to any available space or warehouse under the LESSOR's control with a right of the LESSOR to charge the LESSEE rental for the use of such space or warehouse which rental shall not be more than the rentals for the leased premises.

    It is likewise agreed that should expulsion from the leased premises become necessary, or if after the lapse of fifteen (15) days from the date of expiration or termination for any cause of this lease or eviction, the LESSEE still fail to pay the whole obligation, the LESSOR shall have the right to cause the removal of the LESSEE'S property from the leased premises for said LESSEE'S account, and for this purpose, the LESSOR is hereby constituted as the LESSEE's SPECIAL ATTORNEY-IN-FACT, with the authority and power to sell, dispose or retain any or all of the goods, machinery, furniture, and other equipment and supplies or such portion of the LESSEE'S property as would be sufficient to secure funds to answer for the arrearages, unpaid obligations, the costs of hauling out the property from the leased premises, handling, freight and other charges shall be for the account of the LESSEE.

    This Special Power of Attorney shall continue to subsist for so long as the LESSEE has unpaid obligations arising out of this contract, it being an integral part hereof and the LESSEE hereby expressly gives his consent to and ratifies any and all acts performed by the LESSOR in the exercise of this power.

  22. SURRENDER OF THE LEASED PREMISES

    Upon the expiration or termination of this Contract for any reason whatsoever, the LESSEE shall immediately vacate the Leased Premises, and surrender possession of the leased properties to the LESSOR at the same condition it was received, reasonable wear and tear excepted.

  23. EXCLUSIVE VENUE

    Any dispute regarding the interpretation and implementation of this Contract shall be brought exclusively in the proper Courts of Cebu City only.

  24. WAIVER

    The waiver by either Party of any breach of any term, covenant or condition contained herein shall not be deemed a waiver of any other breach of the same or any other term, covenant or condition hereof.

  25. NON-WAIVER OF CUMULATIVE REMEDIES

    Any failure or delay of the LESSOR in exercising any right, power or remedy under this Contract shall not be construed as a waiver thereof. Nor shall any single or partial exercise of any other rights, power or remedies be construed as the waiver of the other options available to the LESSOR under this contract or under the law; as the LESSEE agrees that the remedies herein provided are cumulative, not exclusive, of any remedies provided by law.

  26. SEVERABILITY

    If any part, term or provision of this Contract shall become invalid or unenforceable, the validity or enforceability of the remaining portions or provisions shall not be affected, and the rights and obligations of the Parties shall be construed as if this Contract did not contain the particular invalid or unenforceable part, term or provision.
