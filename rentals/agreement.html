    The LESSEE shall be responsible for any damage to the property, furniture, and fixtures caused by the LESSEE, household members, or guests.

    If the property is destroyed or rendered uninhabitable by fire, earthquake, typhoon, flood, or other events beyond the control of either party, this lease shall be deemed automatically terminated. Neither party shall be liable to the other for damages resulting from such events.

    The LESSEE agrees that in the event of breach, the LESSOR's remedies under this contract are cumulative and not exclusive of any remedies available under the law.
