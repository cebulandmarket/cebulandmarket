Protecting security deposits for tenants and landlords in Cebu

What is Escrow?

A simple way to protect both tenants and landlords

Escrow means a trusted third party holds money until both sides are satisfied. Instead of sending your security deposit directly to a landlord you haven't met, you send it to CebuRentMarket. We hold it safely via GCash. Once you move in and confirm the unit matches the listing, we release the deposit to the landlord. If the unit doesn't match — you get your money back.

This service is completely FREE. No extra fees for tenants or landlords.

Escrow FAQ

Common questions about our escrow service

How long is the deposit held?

The deposit is held until the tenant moves in and confirms the unit matches the listing. This is typically 1-7 days after the escrow is created, depending on when the move-in is scheduled. If there is no move-in within 30 days, the deposit is returned to the tenant.

What if there's a dispute?

If the tenant says the unit doesn't match the listing, CebuRentMarket will review the situation. We compare the listing photos and description with what the tenant found. If the unit genuinely doesn't match, the deposit is refunded to the tenant. If it does match, the deposit is released to the landlord. Our team makes the final decision based on evidence from both sides.

Is there a fee for using escrow?

No. The escrow service is completely free for both tenants and landlords. There are no extra charges, no service fees, and no hidden costs. We offer this to make renting in Cebu safer for everyone.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept GCash for escrow deposits. Send the deposit to our official GCash number: 0968 751 2330 (account name: CebuLandMarket). Always include the escrow reference ID in your GCash message so we can match the payment.

Is escrow required for all rentals?

No. Escrow is optional. Tenants and landlords can choose to use it for added security, but it's not required. You can still deal directly with each other if both parties are comfortable.

How do I request escrow for my rental?

Simply tell us during the inquiry process that you'd like to use escrow. We'll set it up, provide the reference ID, and guide both parties through the steps. Contact us via Messenger or email.
