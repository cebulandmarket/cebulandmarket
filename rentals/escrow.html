Ask About Escrow

Unsa man ni siya? Basaha ni ha!

Sa normal nga renta, magbayad ka dayon ug deposit sa landlord nga wala pa nimo nakita ang unit — mao na kadaghan ang na-scam. Sa CebuRentMarket, lahi. Kami ang mag-hold sa imong deposit pinaagi sa GCash. Dili namo i-release sa landlord hangtod ikaw mismo ang mo-confirm nga okay ang unit.

Kung ikaw ang tenant (nangita ug abangan):

  • Imbes mag-GCash ka direkta sa landlord, sa CebuRentMarket ka mag-send sa deposit
  • Kami ang mag-hold — dili namo gamiton, dili namo kuhaon
  • Pag-move in nimo, ikaw ang mo-confirm kung okay ba ang unit
  • Kung okay — among i-release ang deposit sa landlord
  • Kung lahi ang unit sa listing — i-refund namo ang imong kwarta, full amount

Kung ikaw ang landlord (tag-iya sa property):

  • Sigurado ka nga tinuod ang tenant kay nag-deposit na siya
  • Pag-confirm sa tenant, ma-receive nimo ang deposit via GCash
  • Dili ka mag-worry kung seryoso ba ang tenant — kung nag-deposit, seryoso na

Libre ra ba ni?

Oo. Libre ra gyud. Walay extra fee ang escrow. Wala mi gi-charge sa landlord o tenant para ani. Gibuhat namo ni para mas safe ang renta sa Cebu para sa tanan.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

How long do you hold the deposit?

Until the tenant moves in and confirms the unit is good. Usually 1-7 days. If there's no move-in within 30 days, the deposit is returned to the tenant automatically.

What if the tenant says the unit is bad but the landlord disagrees?

We review the situation. We compare the listing photos and description with what the tenant found. If the unit genuinely doesn't match, the tenant gets a refund. If it does match, the deposit goes to the landlord. Our team decides based on evidence from both sides.

Is there a fee?

No. Zero. Libre. No extra charges for either side. We offer this for free because we want renting in Cebu to be safer.

How do I pay?

Send the deposit via GCash to 0968 751 2330 (account name: CebuLandMarket). Include your escrow reference ID in the GCash message so we can match your payment.

Is escrow required?

No. It's optional. If both the tenant and landlord are comfortable dealing directly, that's fine. Escrow is there for people who want extra protection.

How do I request escrow?

Just tell us when you inquire about a rental. Say "I want to use escrow" and we'll set it up. Message us on Messenger or email info@cebulandmarket.com.
