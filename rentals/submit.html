Before You Start

Please read and agree to the following terms before submitting your rental listing.

FREE to List — No Upfront Fee!

Listing your rental property on CebuRentMarket is completely FREE. There is no listing fee, no upfront cost, and no monthly charges. You pay nothing to get your property in front of thousands of potential tenants.

A small platform fee is only charged when you successfully find a tenant through our platform. If no tenant is found, you pay nothing.

We'll Create a Property Story for Your Listing

Every approved listing gets its own dedicated page — a property story that shows what makes your rental special. The location, the amenities, the neighborhood. We present it so the right tenant can see what you offer.

A great listing attracts great tenants. Let us help you find the right match.

Document Verification (Due Diligence)

We take verification seriously. Our team reviews your property documents before any listing goes live. This protects tenants and keeps our platform trustworthy. Properties with clean documentation get approved within 24-48 hours.

If your documents are incomplete or have unresolved issues, we will not approve the listing. Since listing is free, there's nothing to refund — zero risk for you.

Not sure if your papers are ready? Contact us first — we'll give you a free assessment before you submit.
