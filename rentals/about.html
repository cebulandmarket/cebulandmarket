About CebuRentMarket

Connecting landlords and tenants across Cebu Province since 2026.

Our Mission

CebuRentMarket was created to make finding and listing rental properties in Cebu easier, more transparent, and more accessible. We believe that every landlord deserves a platform to reach genuine tenants without the high costs of traditional advertising, and every tenant deserves to find a verified, legitimate rental.

We focus on property owners — no agents, no middlemen. Landlords list and manage on their own terms. You keep full control of your property and set your own rent. Tenant inquiries come through the platform and get forwarded directly to you.

What We Do

We provide a trusted online rental listing platform where property owners in Cebu can showcase their houses, condos, apartments, rooms, bedspaces, and commercial spaces for rent. Your property gets its own dedicated page with exposure to thousands of potential tenants searching online — all with verification to keep transactions safe and legitimate.

Your Rental Gets Its Own Page

Every approved listing on CebuRentMarket gets a rental story — a dedicated page just for your property. We don't just post your listing. We share your story. What makes this place special, what tenants can expect, and why it's worth calling home.

Because renting out a property isn't just business. It's sharing a space you care about with someone who'll make it their home. That's what we believe, and that's what we do for every landlord who lists with us.

Why Choose Us?

Direct from Owners: Every rental is listed by the actual property owner or authorized representative. No brokers, no agents, no hidden fees.

For Landlords and Tenants: We make the rental process feel right — not rushed, not shady, not stressful. Landlords get verified tenants. Tenants get verified properties. Everyone wins.

One-Time Payment Only: Our platform fee is a one-time payment — whether short-term or long-term rent. No monthly charges, no recurring fees. Pay once when you successfully find a tenant through CebuRentMarket, and that's it.

Manage Your Listing

Already have an active listing? If you need to update your rent, photos, description, or any details — just contact us and we'll make the changes for you.
